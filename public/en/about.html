    A Journey of Discovery, Learning and Empowerment Through Ice Baths

    Our Story

    SAMUI was born from a personal search for natural ways to improve quality of life, performance and health. Our founders, who worked for years in sports, health and personal development, discovered the amazing power of ice baths when seeking solutions to cope with daily stress and improve athletic performance.

    What started as a personal experiment quickly became a deep passion. After experiencing firsthand the significant changes that ice baths brought to our lives - improved sleep, increased energy levels, enhanced mental clarity and faster recovery from workouts - we realized we had to share this knowledge and experience with others.

    Today, SAMUI is a leading ice bath center in Israel, serving hundreds of participants annually - professional athletes, entrepreneurs, professionals and anyone seeking a natural way to improve their quality of life. We continue to learn, develop and refine our methods, while maintaining our commitment to quality, professionalism and real results.

    Our Mission

    To make the amazing benefits of ice baths accessible to every person in Israel, and provide practical tools for improving health, performance and quality of life through a professional, scientific and personally tailored approach.

    Our Vision

    To be the leading and most trusted ice bath center in Israel, and lead a revolution in the perception of health and lifestyle by combining traditional techniques with current scientific research.

    Our Values

    Professionalism, authenticity, commitment to results, personal approach, continuous learning and maintaining a safe and supportive environment for every participant. We believe in the power of community and mutual support.
